Route overview

A hopscotch journey through Vietnam and China with plenty of city flavor.

Philadelphia → Ho Chi Minh City

Overnight haul with a sunrise arrival to the Mekong gateway.

13.5 hour flight · Land early to banh mi and cà phê sữa đá

  • Stretch out with aisle seats and a cozy hoodie.
  • Plan a quick nap, then a sunset walk along Nguyễn Huệ street.

Ho Chi Minh City → Shenzhen

Scoot north to the Pearl River tech hub.

3 hour flight · Afternoon arrival

  • Grab dim sum near OCT Harbour before the neon comes alive.
  • Check out creative maker markets in Nanshan.

Shenzhen → Chongqing

Into the mountain megacity and spicy heartland.

2.5 hour flight · Evening touchdown

  • First stop: hotpot on Jiefangbei with skyline views.
  • Ride the river cableway at night for the lights show.

Chongqing → Beijing

Capital finale with history, parks, and late-night eats.

3 hour flight · Morning arrival

  • Morning tai chi in Ritan Park, afternoon hutong strolls.
  • Dinner in Sanlitun before a moonlit walk at Houhai.
